All about Lagaw and Learn @ ISUFST
eFARM (Food and Agriculture Resources Management) Academy is a Central Luzon State University (CLSU)-initiated project funded by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) that targets to do the “Transfer-Technology Program” as one of the premiere institutions of agriculture in Southeast Asia. The project, through its eFARM GROW (Generate. Reap. Opportunity. Wealth) component, showcases various food and agriculture technologies developed by the university. In addition, it concomitantly shares different food processing preparations to make the agri-fishery products available for consumption through its eFARM KITCHEN (Knowledge Integration. Culinary Enhancement) component.
